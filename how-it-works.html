How ClearX Works

Our innovative platform connects retailers with excess inventory to budget-conscious shoppers through efficient delivery agents, creating a seamless ecosystem that benefits everyone.

View Process Flow Common Questions

The ClearX Ecosystem

Our platform connects three key stakeholders in a seamless process that reduces waste and saves money.

1. Retailers

Retailers list excess or near-expiry products at discounted prices, helping reduce waste while recovering costs.

2. Customers

Customers browse and purchase discounted products through our app, saving money while helping reduce food waste.

3. Delivery Agents

Delivery agents connect retailers and customers by providing efficient, timely delivery of purchased products.

This three-way ecosystem creates a sustainable solution that benefits all parties while addressing the critical issue of food waste.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about how ClearX works.

ClearX is a platform that connects retailers with excess inventory to budget-conscious shoppers through efficient delivery agents. We help reduce food waste by offering discounted prices on quality products that are approaching their expiration dates.

ClearX generates revenue through a small service fee on each transaction. Retailers pay a commission on sales made through our platform, while customers pay a small service fee and delivery charge. This sustainable business model allows us to maintain and improve our service while keeping costs low for all users.

ClearX is currently available in major metropolitan areas and expanding rapidly. You can download our app or visit our website and enter your location to check if we're operating in your area. If we're not there yet, you can join our waiting list to be notified when we launch near you.

ClearX helps reduce food waste by creating a market for products that would otherwise be discarded due to approaching expiration dates. By connecting retailers with customers willing to purchase these items at discounted prices, we prevent perfectly good food from ending up in landfills. Our platform has already helped save thousands of pounds of food from being wasted.

Absolutely. All food available through ClearX is perfectly safe to consume. The products are simply approaching their best-by or sell-by dates, which are often conservative estimates set by manufacturers. Retailers on our platform maintain high standards for food safety and quality. We recommend consuming or freezing products soon after purchase for optimal quality.

To sign up as a retailer, visit our website or download the ClearX Business app. Click on "Sign Up as Retailer" and follow the registration process. You'll need to provide business details, location information, and complete a verification process. Our team will review your application and typically approve it within 1-3 business days.

You can sell a wide range of food products on ClearX, including fresh produce, dairy, bakery items, meat, seafood, prepared foods, and packaged goods. All products must be safe for consumption and clearly labeled with accurate expiration dates. Non-food perishable items like flowers may also be eligible in some markets.

ClearX operates on a commission-based model, charging a percentage of each sale made through the platform. The standard commission rate is 15%, though this may vary based on volume and product category. There are no upfront costs, monthly fees, or long-term contracts. You only pay when you sell.

Our retailer dashboard provides comprehensive inventory management tools. You can add products individually or use our bulk upload feature for larger inventories. For each product, you'll specify details like regular price, discounted price, expiration date, quantity available, and product images. You can also set up automatic discount tiers based on how close items are to expiration.

Payments are processed weekly for all completed orders. The total amount (minus our commission) is transferred directly to your registered bank account. You can view detailed sales reports and payment history in your dashboard. For high-volume retailers, we offer more frequent payment options.

To place an order, download the ClearX app or visit our website. Create an account, browse available products in your area, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to select a delivery time slot, provide your delivery address, and complete payment. Once your order is confirmed, you can track its status in real-time.

ClearX accepts all major credit and debit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. We also support digital payment methods like Apple Pay, Google Pay, and PayPal. Some markets may offer additional local payment options. All transactions are processed securely through our encrypted payment system.

Delivery fees typically range from 399 to 599, depending on your location and the delivery distance. We offer free delivery on orders above 300 in most areas. During checkout, you'll see the exact delivery fee before confirming your order. ClearX+ members enjoy free delivery on all orders with no minimum purchase requirement.

Customer satisfaction is our priority. If you're not happy with your order, you can report issues through the app or website within 24 hours of delivery. Depending on the situation, we offer refunds, credits, or replacements. While the products are near their best-by dates, they should still meet quality standards. We review each case individually to ensure fair resolution.

Our loyalty program rewards you for every purchase and action on ClearX. You earn points when you place orders, provide ratings and reviews, refer friends, and complete other activities. These points can be redeemed for discounts on future orders, free delivery, or exclusive offers. You can track your points balance and available rewards in the app under "My Rewards."

To become a delivery agent, download the ClearX Delivery app or visit our website and click "Become a Delivery Agent." You'll need to complete an application, provide required documents (ID, driver's license, vehicle registration if applicable), and pass a background check. Once approved, you can set your availability and start accepting delivery requests.

Requirements include being at least 18 years old, having a valid ID, passing a background check, and having access to a reliable mode of transportation (car, bike, scooter, etc.). You'll need a smartphone to run the delivery app and a bank account for payments. In some areas, additional requirements may apply based on local regulations.

Earnings vary based on your location, the number of deliveries you complete, and the distances involved. On average, delivery agents earn 150-250 per hour, including base pay, distance fees, and tips. Our transparent payment structure shows you the exact earning potential before you accept each delivery. Top-performing agents can earn bonuses and incentives during peak hours.

Payments are processed weekly for all completed deliveries. The total amount is transferred directly to your registered bank account. You can view detailed earnings reports in your dashboard. We also offer an Instant Pay feature that allows you to cash out your earnings up to 5 times per day for a small fee.

While a uniform is not required, we recommend wearing clean, professional attire. ClearX provides optional branded t-shirts, caps, and delivery bags that can help you be recognized by retailers and customers. Using these items can improve your delivery experience and may positively impact your ratings and tips.
